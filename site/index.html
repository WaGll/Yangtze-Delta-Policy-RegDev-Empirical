Green Total Factor Productivity, Multi-period DID and Spatial Econometrics

A reproducible research pipeline using Python and Stata for empirical policy evaluation, SBM-GML productivity measurement and spatial econometric analysis.

Project Overview

本项目围绕城市面板数据，构建从原始数据到清洗数据、变量构建、SBM-GML 测算、多期 DID 和空间计量模型的一体化复现流程。
